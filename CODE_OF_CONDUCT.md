# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to keep discussions respectful, practical, and focused on improving the package.

Examples of expected behavior:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different experience levels and viewpoints.
- Give constructive technical feedback.
- Keep issue and pull request discussions on topic.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive comments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other contributions that violate this code of conduct.

Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently restrict participation for repeated or severe violations.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the maintainers. Include relevant links, screenshots, and context so the report can be reviewed.
